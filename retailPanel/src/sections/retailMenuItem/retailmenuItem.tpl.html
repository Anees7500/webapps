DETAILS ABOUT THIS MEAL

Here’s a biriyani that’s good for you! We replaced the empty carbs with some wholesome delicious nutrition. A delicately flavoured melange of quinoa and brown rice meets delicious chunks of malai paneer tikka in this in-house biriyani recipe with a twist of goodness. Served with a delicious raita at the side, this biriyani is all indulgence minus the guilt.
